Who am I?

My name is Iļja Rastopčins. I was born in Rēzekne, I study at the University of Latvia in the 3rd year of the Faculty of Computer Science. I am very interested in the field of IT, at school I was very interested in languages, mathematics, physics. However, in the end I chose mathematics and programming.

I am completely satisfied with my life at the moment and I think that I have grown as a person lately, I hope you will also feel with me, at least in part, some moments that I felt and I will be able to convey the atmosphere of nostalgia that I experienced me, doing a project, collecting old photos, leafing through albums.

While creating the website, I felt like I was writing my biography, thanks to this project I was able to remember many things that I seemed to have forgotten for a long time, but the photos stimulated my memory, and I remembered many things from my childhood that I had forgotten for a long time.

Kindergarten

I studied in the "Varaviksne" kindergarten, where there were several other children in the same group with me, who later became my classmates and friends.

I can't say that I loved that time, because I was often sick, I had problems with self-esteem, but now, looking at that time, I remember it, of course, with warmth and nostalgia.

Interesting facts

Despite being sick often, my mother also taught me at home and I was still one of the most capable children in the group, I could read, write, speak, but my least favorite thing was drawing, because of which I was always in trouble.

In kindergarten, I really liked to play with everyone, I brought my toys, and I also brought a huge collection of "cards", which were quite popular in 2004-2006 and we used to change them. Unfortunately, I left them in the nursery the day I brought them and then forgot about them.

I think kindergarten was a cool time!
